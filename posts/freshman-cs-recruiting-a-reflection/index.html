Freshman CS Recruiting: A Reflection

0 min read

My first semester as a college student has been busy in several different ways. Adjusting to lecture-style classes, having more difficult coursework, and living away from home have all been challenging in different ways.

On the surface, summer internships appear to be the great divide, especially amongst freshman, essentially dividing the "haves" and the "have nots".

In this article I hope to detail my own experience with navigating the recruiting process in order to demystify much of the myths and heresy you may read.
